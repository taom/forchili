Chili

2019 — 2026

Forever our sunshine, forever our good girl

Our Beloved Chili

Chili was a beautiful German Shepherd who came into our lives in 2019 as a tiny, curious puppy with oversized paws and the most soulful brown eyes. Over seven wonderful years, she grew into the most loyal, gentle, and loving companion our family could ever wish for.

She wasn't just a dog — she was the one who greeted us at the door with her whole body wiggling with joy. She was the warm presence beside us on cold nights. She was the patient playmate who let the kids climb all over her. She was the guardian who kept watch over our home. She was, in every way, family.

What Made Chili Special

  • She had the gentlest soul — always so careful and patient around the children
  • Her tail never stopped wagging, even when she was sleepy
  • She could hear a treat bag crinkle from anywhere in the house
  • She loved to "talk" with her adorable little whines and woofs
  • Her favorite spot was right next to whoever was home
  • She believed she was a lap dog, despite weighing 30 kilograms

Chili's Favorite Things

  • Morning walks when the dew was still on the grass
  • Playing fetch until everyone else was tired
  • Belly rubs — she'd roll over for anyone
  • Guarding the family from suspicious squirrels
  • Napping in sunbeams by the window
  • Being wherever her people were

Precious Memories

Seven years of love, joy, and wagging tails

The Day We Met

We remember the day we brought Chili home — a tiny bundle of fur with ears too big for her head. She explored every corner of the house, then fell asleep right in the middle of the living room floor. That was the moment she claimed our home as hers.

The Great Squirrel Chase

Chili took her job as family protector very seriously, especially when it came to squirrels. She once spent an entire afternoon staring up a tree, convinced that if she waited long enough, the squirrel would come down to play.

Bedtime Routine

Every night, Chili would make her rounds — checking on each family member, giving a gentle nudge with her nose, before settling down to sleep. It was her way of saying goodnight, and it made everyone feel so safe and loved.

Snow Day

The first time Chili saw snow, she didn't know what to make of it. Then she started bounding through the white fluff, catching snowflakes on her tongue, rolling on her back. Pure, unbridled joy — that was Chili's way.

The Best Listener

Whenever anyone in the family was having a tough day, Chili always knew. She'd come over quietly, rest her head on your lap, and just be there. No judgment, no questions — just love. She had a gift for making everything feel a little bit better.
